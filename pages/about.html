About

At Umich Closet, we believe there is a better way to sell vintage clothing. A more valuable, less time-intensive way to connect customers to authentic vintage University of Michigan clothing pieces. We are passionate about it and the University to which we owe our business, and strive to connect customers looking for vintage clothing to pieces that we have found and have readily available.
